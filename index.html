Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Nexus?

Simply click the "Add Nexus to Discord" button on our website to invite the bot to your server! Once invited, you can type `/help` to see a full list of commands and get started.

How does the premium subscription work?

You can purchase premium through our website. Once the payment is complete, the premium features for the selected plan (Elite or Prime) are automatically activated on the server you purchased it for.

Can I transfer my premium subscription to another server?

Yes, the original purchaser of a premium plan can transfer it to another server they manage using the `/premium transfer` command.
